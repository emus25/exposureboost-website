Is the wood durable enough for daily use?

Yes. Each card is cut from genuine wood and sealed with a protective finish, so it stands up to everyday life in a wallet or pocket. Wood is a little thicker than plastic, which actually adds to the premium feel — and because the grain is natural, small variations are part of what makes each card unique.

Will it work on my clients' iPhones?

Yes, absolutely. All iPhones from iPhone 7 onwards (iOS 13+) read NFC natively — no app needed. They just hold the card to the top of their phone and your profile opens in Safari instantly. Android phones work the same way from Android 5.0+, which covers virtually every phone made since 2015.

Is the wood sustainably sourced?

Yes. We use responsibly sourced wood, and because your details live on a digital profile you update online, you'll never need to reprint or replace the card when something changes. It's a naturally low-waste choice that fits brands who care about sustainability.

Can I update my profile after the card arrives?

Yes, any time. Log into your dashboard and make any changes — your profile updates immediately and your card taps to the new version right away. Changed jobs, got a new number, launched a new website? Just update your profile. No reprinting, no cost.

Is there a monthly fee or subscription?

No subscription, ever. You pay once for the card and that's it. Your profile is hosted, your updates are free, and your analytics are included — all for the one-time price. We don't charge you to exist.

How do I send you my logo and design?

During checkout you'll upload your logo file (PDF, AI, EPS, or high-res PNG all work). If you don't have a design, you can describe what you want and our team will create a proof for you to approve before we engrave anything. Nothing gets made until you're happy with how it looks.
